Introduction to Tic-Tok-Toe

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe Learning!

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple yet strategic game played on a 3x3 grid. Two players take turns placing their marks (X or O) in the empty squares. The goal is to be the first to get three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How to Play:

  1. Choose your mark: X or O.
  2. Decide who goes first.
  3. Take turns placing your mark in an empty square.
  4. The first player to get three in a row wins!
  5. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a draw.

Strategies:

Consider the following strategies to improve your gameplay:

  • Always start in a corner if you're the first player.
  • Try to create multiple winning opportunities at once.
  • Block your opponent from getting three in a row.
  • Pay attention to the center square; it offers more winning paths.

Enjoy the game!
