Welcome to Sudoku Learning!

Sudoku is a logic-based number placement puzzle that has gained immense popularity worldwide. It challenges players to fill a 9x9 grid with digits, ensuring that each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid contains all digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.

In this section, you'll learn the basics of Sudoku, various strategies to solve puzzles efficiently, and advanced techniques to tackle more challenging grids. Each topic is designed to help you enhance your skills and enjoy the game.
